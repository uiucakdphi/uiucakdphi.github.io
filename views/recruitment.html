Fall 2016 Rush | Run Home to alpha Kappa Delta Phi

What is Rush?

Rush is the time when girls who are interested in joining alpha Kappa Delta Phi here at UIUC can come out to various events and meet the sisters of aKDPhi! All rush events are free and there is no obligation to join if you attend.
