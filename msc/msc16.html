To our lovely Midwest sisters,

On behalf of Upsilon Chapter, I am ecstatic to welcome everyone to this year's Spring 2016 Midwest Sisterhood Conference hosted at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Located in a campus-based town, this city thrives on the energy emanating from the students. Although we are located in the middle of nowhere, surrounded by lots and lots of corn, we hope that this weekend provides everyone with the opportunity to create unforgettable memories while forging everlasting friendships. Whether this is your first or last MSC, we hope you enjoy your time here!
